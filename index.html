NCC Email Signature Client Proof of Concept

Outlook Add-in — Nambour Christian College

Auto-inserts a branded NCC email signature into Outlook compose windows, pulling each staff member's name and role directly from their M365 profile. Staff can customise their sign-off and extension, and their preferences follow them across devices.
